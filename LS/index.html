Thomas P. Kennard House

Nebraska Statehood Memorial

The oldest remaining building in Lincoln’s original plat. Built for Nebraska’s first Secretary of State to prove the city’s viability as a capital.

Lewis Syford House

Second-Empire Style Gem

A rare example of 1870s residential architecture in Lincoln, preserved as a testament to the city’s early growth and expansion.

The oldest surviving building on the University of Nebraska-Lincoln campus. Originally built as the university library, it now anchors the College of Architecture.
