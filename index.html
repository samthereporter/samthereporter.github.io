About Me

I'm a junior in the College of Journalism and Mass Communications at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln (UNL), where I've consistently made the Dean's List. My focus is on grassroots community reporting, social impact, and civic accountability.

I currently serve as the President of the Multicultural Students in Media Club, where we work to foster a more welcoming and diverse community within our industry. I've also had the privilege of reporting on the ground for Civic Nebraska during the 2023 Lincoln primary, interviewing voters about why local elections matter.

This site serves as my professional portfolio, hosting both my published journalistic work and a collection of miscellaneous web apps and experiments I'm building. Feel free to look around.

P.S. If you're looking for the Los Angeles-based magician and escape artist, that's the other Sam Mueggenberg. I write stories and build apps, but I haven't mastered escaping from a straitjacket... yet.
